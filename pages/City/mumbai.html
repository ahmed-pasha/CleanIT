City Overview

Mumbai is the capital of Maharashtra, India. It is known for its rich history and thriving technology sector. It's famous for its iconic beach and many Bollywood Stars and Producers.


Mumbai faces critical challenges with waste and pollution, impacting its natural beauty, marine life, and air quality.


The city's dense population and diverse industries contribute to mounting waste issues, with landfill sites reaching capacity and air pollution levels rising significantly.
