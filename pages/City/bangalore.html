City Overview

Bangalore, also called Bengaluru, is the capital of Karnataka, India.It is known for its pleasant weather all year round. The city is often called the "Silicon Valley of India" because it is a major hub for IT companies.


Beyond its role as an IT hub, Bangalore faces growing concerns with waste production and pollution, affecting both its environment and quality of life.


Despite its modern infrastructure, the city struggles with waste management and pollution, posing significant challenges to its sustainable growth.
