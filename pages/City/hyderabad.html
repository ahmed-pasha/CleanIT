City Overview

Hyderabad is the capital of Telengana, India. It is known for its rich history and thriving technology sector. It's famous for its iconic Charminar monument and delectable biryani cuisine


Hyderabad faces significant waste management challenges, with efforts focused on curbing pollution through sustainable practices and public awareness initiatives.


The city’s rapid growth has highlighted the need for efficient waste disposal methods, making waste management a priority for both residents and authorities.
