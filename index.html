About Us

CleanIT is a community-driven platform empowering citizens to report and address waste issues in their local areas. Through a simple interface, users can file complaints about waste problems, and CleanIT connects them with verified local waste management services for quick and effective solutions.



Our mission is to promote cleaner, healthier communities by rewarding proactive citizens. CleanIT makes it easy to report waste issues, offering incentives and rewards for participation, and providing a platform where every user’s contribution counts toward a cleaner environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

CleanIT connects you with local cleanup professionals, called EcoMates, who respond to waste reports in your area. EcoMates visit the reported locations, clean up the waste, and earn payment through our platform.

EcoMates handle a variety of tasks, including waste removal, neighborhood cleanups, and special waste management projects, providing a cleaner, more sustainable community.

When a user reports a waste issue, the task is added to the EcoMates’ platform. Available EcoMates can accept the task, visit the site, and complete the cleanup.

CleanIT currently operates in select cities, but we’re working to expand to more locations to support communities nationwide.

EcoMates receive payment for each task they complete, funded through user subscriptions. CleanIT ensures fair compensation for EcoMates’ efforts in maintaining community cleanliness.

CleanIT operates on a subscription model, where users pay a monthly fee to report waste issues. This subscription supports EcoMates and keeps our platform running efficiently.
